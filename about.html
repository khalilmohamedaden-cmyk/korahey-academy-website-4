Our Beginning

Korahey Academy opened its doors in 2008 with 45 students and 6 teachers. Founded on the belief that quality education should be accessible to every family in Jijiga, it quickly became one of the most trusted institutions in the region. What started in a single building has grown into a full campus serving over 1,200 students.

Our Commitment

From primary through senior secondary, every programme at Korahey is designed around the whole child — academic rigour balanced with character development, faith, and community. Our graduates don't just pass exams. They lead, they serve, and they succeed. That is the Korahey promise.

Our Journey

Milestones That
Shaped Us

From a small community classroom to a leading institution — every year has been a step forward.

2008 — Foundation

The First Bell Rings

Korahey Academy opens with 45 students and 6 teachers, offering primary education rooted in academic excellence and Islamic values.

2012 — Expansion

Secondary School Opens

Grades 9–12 are introduced alongside a dedicated science laboratory, giving students a complete pathway from primary to university.

2016 — Innovation

Digital Classrooms

Computer labs and technology-integrated learning transform how students engage with knowledge, preparing them for a modern world.

2020 — Growth

New Campus Wing

A major expansion adds a library, sports facilities, and modern classrooms — built to serve a growing generation of learners.

2023 — Achievement

98% University Rate

Korahey graduates are accepted to top universities across Ethiopia and abroad, cementing the school's reputation for excellence.

Today — Legacy

Over 1,200 Strong

Korahey Academy stands as Jijiga's leading institution — 1,200+ students, 85+ educators, and a community proud of what we've built together.
