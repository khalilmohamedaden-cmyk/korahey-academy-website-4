Our Journey

A Legacy of Excellence in Jijiga

Korahey Academy was founded in 2008 with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide world-class education rooted in local values. What started as a small community school has grown into one of the most respected institutions in the region, serving hundreds of students from Jijiga and beyond.

Milestones

Our Journey Through the Years

From a humble beginning to a leading educational institution.

2008

Foundation

Korahey Academy opened its doors with just 45 students and 6 teachers, offering primary education with a focus on academic excellence and Islamic values.

2012

Secondary Expansion

Introduced secondary education (Grades 9-12) and built a dedicated science laboratory to enhance practical learning for students.

2016

Digital Transformation

Launched computer labs and integrated technology into the curriculum, preparing students for the modern world.

2020

New Campus Wing

Completed a major campus expansion, adding a library, sports facilities, and modern classrooms to accommodate growing enrollment.

2023

University Success

Achieved 98% university acceptance rate with graduates admitted to top universities across Ethiopia and abroad.

Today

Leading Institution

Korahey Academy stands as a beacon of quality education in Jijiga, serving over 1200 students with a dedicated team of 85+ educators.

What We Believe

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Korahey Academy.

Academic Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in teaching and learning, ensuring every student reaches their full potential.

Respect & Integrity

We foster a culture of honesty, mutual respect, and ethical behavior among students, staff, and parents.

Safe & Supportive

Every child deserves a secure environment where they feel valued, heard, and encouraged to grow.

Innovation

We embrace modern teaching methods, technology, and creative approaches to prepare students for the future.

Community Engagement

We believe in strong partnerships between school, families, and the wider Jijiga community.

Character Building

Education goes beyond academics — we nurture responsible, compassionate, and confident young leaders.

Why Choose Us

More Than Just a School

At Korahey Academy, we believe education is the bridge between dreams and reality. Our holistic approach combines strong academics, character development, and a nurturing environment that empowers every student to become their best self.

With experienced teachers, modern facilities, and a curriculum that balances national standards with local values, we prepare students not just for exams, but for life.
